Welcome to The Garage

The Garage is a platform for employee-led innovation at Microsoft. It's an online community of thousands of employees and interns around the globe who are passionate about turning their wild ideas into working hardware or software prototypes. There are Garage facilities in 12 locations worldwide (Redmond; Vancouver; Silicon Valley; Boston; Herzliya, Israel; Hyderabad, India; and Beijing, China).
